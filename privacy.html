Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Mise ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. We collect minimal personal data necessary to provide our recipe organization, meal planning, and grocery tracking services. This may include account information if you choose to sync via iCloud, crash logs, and anonymized app usage data.

  • iCloud Sync: If enabled, your recipes and meal plans are synced across your devices using Apple's iCloud infrastructure. We do not have direct access to this data.
  • Usage Data: We collect non-personally identifiable information automatically to improve performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect strictly to operate, maintain, and enhance the Mise application. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Mise allows you to import recipes from third-party websites. Note that when you browse other websites using our import tool, their privacy policies and terms apply. We are not responsible for the data collection practices of external websites.

4. Data Retention and Security

We prioritize the security of your data. As most user-generated data (such as recipes) is tied directly to your Apple ID via iCloud, it benefits from Apple's robust security protocols. We retain collected usage metrics only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by publishing the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us by visiting the Support page.
