Features

Everything you need to cook with confidence.

📖

Every recipe you love. One place to find them.

Import from any URL — food blogs, Instagram saves, that recipe your friend texted you at midnight. Organize with categories, tags, and star ratings so your go-tos are always within reach.

🔥

Hands busy? We've got you.

Step-by-step instructions, built-in timers, and a dark kitchen-friendly display that stays out of your way while you cook. No scrolling through life stories. Just the next step.

📅

Plan your week in minutes, not hours.

Drag recipes into a weekly calendar. Or let Mise's AI do it for you — it considers variety, cook time, and what you actually like to eat. Sunday planning sessions just got a lot shorter.

🛒

From plan to cart, automatically.

Your meal plan generates a grocery list. Ingredients are consolidated (no buying cilantro three times) and organized by aisle. Tap to check off as you shop.

📲

Switching apps? Bring everything with you.

Paste any recipe URL and Mise extracts it instantly. Coming from Paprika? Import your entire library in one tap. Your collection stays yours.

☁️

Start on your phone, finish on your iPad.

With Mise Pro, your recipes, meal plans, and grocery lists sync across all your Apple devices. Plan on the couch, cook from the counter.
