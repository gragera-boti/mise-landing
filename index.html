Your kitchen companion

From discovering a recipe to putting dinner on the table, Cocotte handles every step.

Import

Paste a link, get a recipe.

Drop in a URL from any recipe site — AllRecipes, Bon Appetit, Food Network, NYT Cooking — and Cocotte extracts everything: ingredients, steps, photos, and cook times. Migrating from Paprika? Import your entire archive in one tap.

  • Supports 1,000+ recipe websites
  • Paprika archive import
  • Smart ingredient parsing
Cook

Hands-free, step by step.

Cooking Mode transforms recipes into a distraction-free walkthrough. Large text, dark background for kitchen visibility, and built-in timers that keep you on track. Swipe through steps — no scrolling, no squinting.

  • Dark UI optimized for kitchens
  • Built-in step timers
  • Swipe between steps
Plan

Your week, planned in minutes.

Drag recipes into a weekly calendar — breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks. Need inspiration? AI-powered auto-planning suggests meals based on variety, cook time, and your dietary preferences.

  • Weekly calendar grid
  • AI-powered meal suggestions (Pro)
  • Drag-and-drop simplicity
Shop

From plan to cart, automatically.

Generate grocery lists from any recipe or your entire meal plan. Ingredients are consolidated (no duplicate onions!) and categorized by aisle — produce, dairy, pantry, proteins. Tap to check off as you shop.

  • Auto-generated from meal plans
  • Smart ingredient consolidation
  • Organized by category
Collect

Your recipes, beautifully organized.

Every recipe lives in one place — browsable by category, searchable by ingredient, sortable by rating. Rich detail pages with full-bleed food photos, scaling servings with a tap, and all the metadata that makes a recipe yours.

  • Categories, tags, and star ratings
  • Full-bleed food photography
  • Instant recipe scaling
